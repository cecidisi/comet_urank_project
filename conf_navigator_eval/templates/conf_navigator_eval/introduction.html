Welcome to the study of uRank Conference Navigator! WE appreciate you are taking the time to take part in it. Your participation will help our group to evaluate and improve our application and make it more useful for future conference attendees. Anything you do or say will be kept strictly anonymous and confidential, so please feel free to explore the system and provide any kind of feedback.

uRank Conference Navigator is a recommender system that aims at helping you find interesting papers at UMAP 2017. We combine information from papers' content and user profiles and present it in a visual inteface. The UI allows you to explore features (keywords and other users) and discover relevant papers for you.

The study consists in 4 tasks. The task goal is to bookmark 2 papers using a different variation of the UI. The following 4 variations will. be presented to you in random order:

  • Content-based Recommender (CB)
  • Social-based Recommender (SB)
  • Parallel Content- + Social-based Recommender (Par CB // SB)
  • Hybrid Content- + Social-based Recommender (Hyb CB + SB)

Please think out loud while you attempt these tasks and ask questions when needed.
